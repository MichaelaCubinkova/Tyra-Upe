Tyra Upė is a mother-daughter project aiming to encourage women to choose nature-friendly reusable hygiene products. Our mission is to break the myth that intimate reusables are complicated – we found comfort in simplicity and believe that reusables are worthy to become a natural habit of each woman. We believe that big changes happen with small everyday choices and each little one matters.
